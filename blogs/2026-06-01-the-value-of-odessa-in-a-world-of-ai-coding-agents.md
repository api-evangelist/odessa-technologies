The article argues that while AI coding tools have improved developer productivity, specialized domain expertise remains irreplaceable in complex industries like asset finance. Odessa contends that building lease and loan origination software requires deep understanding of credit decisioning, regulatory compliance, tax treatments, and operational workflows — knowledge accumulated over 27 years of industry focus. The piece emphasizes that judgment, accountability, and long-term partnership with clients cannot be replicated by AI agents alone.
